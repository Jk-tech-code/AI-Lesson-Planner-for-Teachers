# **Research Notes: Why AI Writing Feels Fake**

## **Executive Summary**

The strongest finding across research is that AI writing usually does **not** feel fake because of one specific word or phrase.

Instead, readers notice a combination of:

* Predictable structure  
* Repetitive language patterns  
* Excessive smoothness  
* Lack of genuine perspective  
* Uniform sentence rhythm  
* Generic examples  
* Low surprise factor

Researchers repeatedly note that AI-generated text is often grammatically excellent while lacking the irregularity and individuality of human writing.

# **1\. Predictability Is the Biggest Tell**

Most studies describe AI text as highly predictable.

Common characteristics:

* Expected word choices  
* Expected sentence structures  
* Expected paragraph organization  
* Few unexpected observations

Human writers frequently:

* Change direction  
* Add unusual examples  
* Introduce personal opinions  
* Break patterns

AI tends to avoid those risks.

# **2\. AI Is Optimized for Probability, Not Perspective**

AI predicts the most likely next word.

Humans communicate experiences, opinions, beliefs, frustrations, and observations.

As a result:

### **AI Writing**

* Technically correct  
* Polished  
* Generic

### **Human Writing**

* Messy  
* Specific  
* Opinionated  
* Memorable

One researcher summarized the difference as:

AI is optimized for plausibility, not personality.
